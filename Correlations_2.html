Purpose

The purpose of this slideshow is introduce the concept of correlations and to discuss how they function both mathematically and visually.

Correlation

A correlation describes the linear relationship between two continuous variables. In practical terms, the correlation between two variables reflects the degree to which variables systematically change with each other.
